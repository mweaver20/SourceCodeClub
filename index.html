About Our Club

Our purpose in running the Source Code Club is to give students learning any type of coding/programming, a community to turn to! Our meetings include discussions and lessons on a variety of programming concepts. We also now have an Instagram account! Click below to check us out!

How to join

We are always Looking for new members! Our club mostly operates through our Discord server so joining is easy! Here we have many different channels for all members to enjoy and interact with other members! All club meetings are hosted on the Discord as well!

Meeting schedule

All meetings take place once a week on our Discord server! We meet on Thursdays from 6:00 to 8:00 PM. While we are currently on break for the summer semester, we will be returning this Fall for all new meetings! Meetings will also be recorded and posted below for your convenience!
